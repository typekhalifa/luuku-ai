# Luuku AI

Luuku AI is a Rwanda-based AI systems architecture and consulting company focused on designing, building, and deploying reliable AI systems for businesses, institutions, and the African ecosystem.

## Current Mission
Build Luuku AI from the inside out by first creating our own internal AI Operating System:
- Executive Assistant Agent
- Research Agent
- Sales Agent
- Knowledge System
- Agent Orchestration Layer

## Vision
To become one of Africa’s most trusted AI systems companies, helping organizations implement AI that is practical, reliable, and scalable.

## Current Version
**v0.1 — Internal AI Workforce Foundation**
