# Founder Profile — Luuku AI

## Company
Luuku AI

## Company Description
Luuku AI is a Rwanda-based AI systems architecture and consulting company focused on designing, building, and deploying practical AI systems for businesses, institutions, and the African ecosystem.

## Mission
Build Luuku AI into one of Africa’s strongest AI systems companies by creating practical AI infrastructure, internal AI workforces, and client-facing AI solutions.

## Current Strategic Priorities
- Build Luuku AI’s internal AI operating system
- Develop a strong AI systems architecture consulting positioning
- Research client opportunities in Rwanda and Africa
- Build AI agents that can support company operations
- Improve founder execution, planning, and focus
- Move from experimentation into serious technical delivery

## Founder Working Style

## What Counts as High-Value Work
The Executive Assistant should prioritize work that:
1. builds Luuku AI as a serious company
2. improves technical capability in AI systems architecture
3. creates client opportunities, revenue, or market positioning
4. strengthens internal systems, agents, and operating processes
5. reduces confusion and helps the founder execute clearly
